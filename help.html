How The Game Is Played !

A W D ▭

Left Arrow Or A Key: Moves Left
Right Arrow Or D Key: Moves Right
Left Mouse Click: Snowball Attack
Shift Key: Dash
Spacebar or W Key: Jumps in Position

Combination Moves




Moving Jump: Spacebar (Hold) + Arrow (Left or Right)
Air Dash: Spacebar (Hold) + Arrow (Left or Right) + D Key
Air Snowball: Spacebar (Hold) + Arrow (Left or Right) + LMS Click
