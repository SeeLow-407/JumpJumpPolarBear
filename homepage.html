Welcome to Jump, Jump, Polarbear!

Welcome to our game!

Our game address the environmental issue of climate change which is melting ice caps and the displaced polar bears throughout the north pole. This is a severe problem to address as it endangers the wildlife of the north pole, losing natural habitats and may even cause an entire ecosystem to be destroyed resulting in extinction of a population of animals.




The game objective is to get a family of polar bears that were split apart by cracking ice caps. They must run, jump from ice cap to ice cap to reunite with each other. Prevent them from being displaced and reunite them to fix a seperated family caused by disastourous circumstances. Can you do it? Good Luck.
